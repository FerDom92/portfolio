## Role Overview

At Grupo CMIC, I specialized in building healthcare and hospital management systems, focusing on creating efficient solutions for HR, patient tracking, and hospital operations.

## Key Projects

### HR Personnel Tracking System
- Developed a comprehensive human resources system with payroll management
- Implemented leave management and personnel tracking features
- Built with Vue.js, Node.js, MySQL, and Sails.js
- Streamlined HR operations for healthcare facilities

### Healthcare Personnel Monitoring
- Created a monitoring system for healthcare personnel and stretcher-bearers
- Real-time tracking and management capabilities
- Technologies: Vue.js, Node.js, MySQL, Sails.js
- Improved operational efficiency and resource allocation

### Patient Traceability System
- Developed a patient traceability system for pre-surgery workflows
- Enhanced patient safety and surgical preparation processes
- Stack: Vue.js, Node.js, Sails.js, MySQL
- Improved coordination between departments

### Purchase Orders & Engineering Tasks Management
- Built a real-time system to manage hospital purchase orders
- Monitored hospital engineering tasks with live updates
- Implemented WebSockets and Socket.IO for real-time communication
- Technologies: Vue.js, Node.js, Sails.js, WebSockets, Socket.IO
- Enhanced transparency and task coordination

## Technologies Used

Vue.js, Node.js, Sails.js, MySQL, WebSockets, Socket.IO

## Impact

Successfully delivered multiple critical healthcare management systems that improved operational efficiency, patient care, and resource management across hospital facilities.
